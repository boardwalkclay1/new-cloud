Safety Cloud

The Beltline’s digital safety layer — built to keep everyone moving smoothly.

What is Safety Cloud?

Safety Cloud is the Beltline’s official guide for safe movement, respectful parking, and community awareness. It was created by Beltline Cloud to help riders, walkers, skaters, runners, and vendors share the trail without blocking, crowding, or causing accidents.

Inside the full Beltline Cloud app, Safety Cloud includes:

  • ✔ A live map showing safe parking zones
  • ✔ Areas where you should NOT park or block the trail
  • ✔ Courtesy guidelines for riders, walkers, and skaters
  • ✔ Community alerts and safety updates
  • ✔ Event locations and safe meetup spots
  • ✔ Night riding safety tips and visibility zones

This public page gives you the full breakdown of how to move safely on the Beltline — whether you’re a Cloud user or not.

Parking & Stopping Zones

The Beltline is a shared space. Parking or stopping in the wrong areas can block traffic, cause collisions, and disrupt the flow of movement. Safety Cloud helps you know exactly where to stop.

✔ Safe Parking Zones

These areas are wide, open, and designed for stopping without blocking movement.

  • • Trail edges with wide shoulders
  • • Designated rest areas
  • • Vendor-approved pickup zones
  • • Event meetup spots

✘ Do NOT Park Here

These areas create dangerous bottlenecks and block the natural flow of the Beltline.

  • • Narrow trail sections
  • • Blind corners
  • • Bridge entrances/exits
  • • Directly in front of vendors
  • • Middle of the trail (never stop here)

Inside the Cloud app, the map highlights these zones visually so you always know where to stop safely.

Movement & Courtesy Guidelines

The Beltline is a flow-based environment. Safety Cloud helps everyone move together without collisions or frustration.

Riders & Skaters

  • • Stay to the right unless passing
  • • Call out “On your left!” when passing
  • • Slow down near families or crowds
  • • Use lights at night
  • • Never weave unpredictably

Walkers & Runners

  • • Stay aware of faster traffic
  • • Keep pets close to avoid collisions
  • • Avoid stopping in narrow areas
  • • Move to the side when checking your phone

Vendors

  • • Keep setups tight and off the main trail
  • • Use designated vendor zones
  • • Avoid blocking movement with tables or signs
  • • Use Fast Roll pickup zones for deliveries

Night Riding Safety

The Beltline transforms at night — and Safety Cloud helps keep it safe for everyone.

  • ✔ Use bright lights (front + rear)
  • ✔ Wear reflective clothing or LED straps
  • ✔ Avoid dark, unlit sections
  • ✔ Ride in pairs when possible
  • ✔ Slow down — visibility drops dramatically
  • ✔ Stay aware of pedestrians who may not see you

Inside the Cloud app, night visibility zones are highlighted on the map.

Community Alerts

Safety Cloud keeps you informed about:

  • ✔ Trail closures
  • ✔ Construction zones
  • ✔ High-traffic events
  • ✔ Weather hazards
  • ✔ Lost items or pets
  • ✔ Emergency situations

Cloud users receive real-time alerts inside the app, and vendors/riders get updates relevant to their routes and locations.

Emergency Information

If you encounter an emergency on the Beltline:

  • ✔ Call 911 immediately
  • ✔ Move yourself and others out of the main trail
  • ✔ Notify nearby vendors or Cloud users
  • ✔ Use Safety Cloud to mark the incident location

Inside the Cloud app, emergency markers help responders and community members locate incidents faster.
