What is Safety Cloud?

Safety Cloud is here purely for the Beltline community’s safety. It exists to make the Beltline a safer, more respectful, and more enjoyable place for everyone — riders, walkers, skaters, runners, families, vendors, and visitors.

Our mission is simple: Do everything in our power to keep the Beltline safe, informed, and moving smoothly.

Safety Cloud explains:

  • ✔ Beltline etiquette and courtesy rules
  • ✔ How to move safely with riders, walkers, and skaters
  • ✔ Where to park and where NOT to park
  • ✔ The Beltline pole marker system (emergency location numbers)
  • ✔ The Beltline emergency sticker system
  • ✔ Night riding safety and visibility
  • ✔ Community alerts and hazard updates
  • ✔ How Safety Cloud works inside Beltline Cloud

Some topics — like the emergency marker system, courtesy rules, and parking map — will have their own dedicated pages inside the Cloud for deeper detail.

Beltline Etiquette & Courtesy

The Beltline is a shared movement space. Safety Cloud teaches the etiquette that keeps everyone safe:

Movement Flow

  • • Walkers stay to the right
  • • Riders, skaters, and wheels pass through the middle
  • • Always call out “On your left!” when passing
  • • Keep pets close and avoid long leashes
  • • Move to the side when stopping or checking your phone

Respect & Awareness

  • • Treat everyone with kindness — we’re all here to enjoy the Beltline
  • • Slow down near families, crowds, or tight spaces
  • • Avoid weaving unpredictably
  • • Be aware of headphones — you may not hear riders approaching

Walking Courtesy

  • • Walkers stay right
  • • If walking as a couple, the man walks on the inside (trail side)
  • • Keep groups tight — avoid blocking the trail

A full “Beltline Courtesy Guide” page will be available inside the Cloud.

Parking & Stopping Zones

Parking incorrectly is one of the biggest causes of accidents and blocked movement on the Beltline. Safety Cloud teaches exactly where to stop — and where not to.

✔ Safe Parking Zones

  • • Wide trail edges
  • • Designated rest areas
  • • Vendor pickup zones
  • • Event meetup spots

✘ Do NOT Park Here

  • • Narrow trail sections
  • • Blind corners
  • • Bridge entrances/exits
  • • Directly in front of vendors
  • • Middle of the trail (never stop here)

The Safety Cloud Parking Map will have its own dedicated page inside the Cloud.

Beltline Emergency Marker System

The Beltline uses a pole number system — small stickers placed on trail poles that tell emergency responders exactly where you are.

Safety Cloud teaches you:

  • ✔ What the stickers look like

  • ✔ How to report your location using the number
  • ✔ How APD Path Force uses the marker system
  • ✔ What to do if you can’t find a pole

The full Marker System Guide will have its own page inside the Cloud.

Night Riding Safety

The Beltline transforms at night — and visibility becomes the #1 safety factor.

  • ✔ Use bright lights (front + rear)
  • ✔ Wear reflective clothing or LED straps
  • ✔ Avoid dark, unlit sections
  • ✔ Ride in pairs when possible
  • ✔ Slow down — visibility drops dramatically
  • ✔ Stay aware of pedestrians who may not see you

Night Visibility Zones will have their own page inside the Cloud.

Emergency Information

If you encounter an emergency on the Beltline:

  • ✔ Call 911 immediately
  • ✔ Move yourself and others out of the main trail
  • ✔ Notify nearby vendors or Cloud users
  • ✔ Use Safety Cloud to mark the incident location
