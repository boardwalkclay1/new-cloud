About Boardwalk Clay

Longboard dancer. Creator. Builder. Beltline storyteller.

The Journey

Boardwalk Clay began as a skater with a board, a camera, and a mission: to capture the rhythm, movement, and soul of the Atlanta Beltline.

What started as longboard dancing evolved into a lifestyle — a way to connect people, uplift the community, and bring energy to the spaces we share.

After years of skating the Beltline — sunrise rides, late‑night sessions, community meetups, and thousands of miles — the vision grew into something bigger.

Years on the Beltline

The Beltline isn’t just a path — it’s a living, breathing part of Atlanta. Boardwalk Clay has spent years skating it, documenting it, and connecting with the people who make it special.

From Old Fourth Ward to Piedmont Park, from Krog Street to the Westside Trail, the Beltline has shaped our movement, creativity, and mission.

The Mission — Boardwalk Beltline Inc.

Boardwalk Beltline Inc. exists to teach, build, and uplift the community through movement, creativity, and communication.

Our mission is to make the Beltline safer, more connected, and more informed.

• Public safety communication

• Community education

• Creative expression

• Beltline culture preservation

• Youth empowerment

Teaching & Community Building

Through workshops, events, and hands‑on sessions, Boardwalk Clay teaches longboard dancing, flow, and freestyle expression.

But it goes deeper than skating — it’s about confidence, discipline, creativity, and community.
