The Beltline Cloud

A Beltline‑native ecosystem built for movement, safety, creativity, and community.

The Cloud

The Beltline Cloud is the digital heartbeat of the Beltline — a unified platform designed to connect skaters, riders, creators, vendors, event hosts, and the entire Beltline community.

Built by the Cloud Creator, the system brings together safety, communication, movement, and culture into one seamless experience. It’s more than an app — it’s the infrastructure for the future of the Beltline.

• Real-time safety alerts

• Beltline location detection

• Fast Roll client & rider system

• Vendor marketplace

• Event hosting & ticketing

• Response Unit coordination

• Community communication tools

Fast Roll

Fast Roll is the Beltline’s own movement network — a native transportation system built for riders, skaters, and flow movers. Clients can request rides, and riders can go active directly through the app.

It’s fast, fluid, and designed specifically for the Beltline’s unique culture and pathways.

The Response Unit

The Response Unit is one of the most important parts of the Cloud — a community-powered safety and communication system built for Beltline natives.

We are the response. Made for us, by us.

Members can join groups, track activity, coordinate support, and help keep the Beltline safe and informed.

Events & Ticketing

The Cloud makes it easy to throw events directly through the app — from workshops to meetups to full-scale Beltline gatherings.

Hosts can create events, manage details, and sell tickets instantly. Attendees can purchase tickets directly through the app with a single tap.

Vendors & Marketplace

The vendor system empowers Beltline creators, food sellers, artists, and service providers to build their brand inside the Cloud.

Vendors can showcase products, accept orders, and connect with the community — all from their Beltline-native profile.

The Expansion Vision

The Beltline Cloud is only the beginning. The mission is to expand across Atlanta — connecting neighborhoods, parks, trails, and communities.

The future includes more routes, more vendors, more events, more safety tools, and more ways to move.

The Cloud is built to grow — and built to last.
