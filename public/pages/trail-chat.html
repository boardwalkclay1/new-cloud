Trail Chat Safety Guide

Safety is the first priority on Trail Chat. This page exists to keep conversation aligned with real‑world safety on the Beltline. When people interact here, they are expected to respect the trail, each other, and the law.

  • ✔ No harassment, threats, or unsafe behavior coordination.
  • ✔ Do not encourage speeding, collisions, or reckless riding.
  • ✔ Respect families, kids, elders, and pedestrians at all times.
  • ✔ Use Trail Chat to support safety, not undermine it.
  • ✔ Report emergencies by calling 911 — Trail Chat is not an emergency line.
  • ✔ Follow Safety Cloud etiquette, rules, and marker system guidance.

Trail Chat is part of Safety Cloud. It exists to make the Beltline safer, more connected, and more aware — not more chaotic. Every message should move the Beltline in a safer direction.

For full safety details, visit Safety Cloud.
