Rider Agreement

You ride at your own risk. The Fast Roll is a connector between clients and riders, not a carrier, employer, or insurer. You are responsible for your own safety, your equipment, and your interactions with clients.

You agree to obey traffic laws, respect pedestrians, and ride with control on the Beltline. Any disputes about orders are between you and the client. The Fast Roll may suspend or remove riders based on complaints, safety concerns, or repeated bad reviews.
