How Being a Rider Works

The Fast Roll is built for Beltline‑native movement: longboards, skates, e‑bikes, OneWheels. You’re not a courier company employee — you’re an independent rider choosing which jobs to accept.

1. Set your radius

In your dashboard, set the radius you’re comfortable riding. Jobs shown to you are meant to stay inside that zone once the map engine is wired in.

2. Accept jobs

You’ll see available jobs with pickup and dropoff spots. When you accept a job, it becomes your active delivery. You’re responsible for getting there safely and on time.

3. Pickup + proof

At pickup, you grab the item and upload a quick photo. This keeps everyone honest and protects you.

4. Dropoff + proof

At dropoff, you hand off the item and upload a photo. Once marked delivered, the system logs your time and updates your stats.

5. Payouts

Payments flow through PayPal. The Fast Roll takes 25% of the delivery fee; the rest is yours. Tips go straight to you.

6. Safety + community

The Beltline is tight. Ride with respect. If you rack up 5 bad reviews, your account is suspended until reviewed. Clients can file complaints; you can respond, but The Fast Roll is not liable for disputes between you and clients.
