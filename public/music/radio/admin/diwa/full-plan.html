Beltline Radio — Full Operational Plan

The official blueprint for running Beltline Radio on Radio.co

1. Mission & Identity

Beltline Radio is the soundtrack of the Beltline lifestyle — movement, culture, skating, community, and the Cloud ecosystem.

2. Platform Setup (Radio.co)

  • Create Radio.co broadcaster account
  • Upload music library
  • Build AutoDJ playlists
  • Set station schedule
  • Configure branding
  • Embed player into Beltline Cloud

3. AutoDJ Playlist Structure

  • Morning Flow
  • Afternoon Energy
  • Evening Vibes
  • Late Night Cloud
  • Weekend Mixes
  • Special Events

4. Live DJ Mode

Used for events, interviews, vendor showcases, and special nights. AutoDJ resumes automatically when live mode ends.

5. Website Integration

Embed the Radio.co player into Beltline Cloud dashboards, vendor pages, Fast Roll rider screens, and The Network Explore page.

6. Analytics & Growth

Use Radio.co analytics to adjust playlists, schedule shows, sell sponsorships, and grow the community.

7. Revenue Streams

  • Vendor sponsorships
  • Paid ad slots
  • Radio merch
  • Memberships
  • Event promotions

8. Integration With Beltline Cloud

Beltline Radio connects to The Network, Fast Roll, Safety Cloud, Cumulus Cloud, vendor dashboards, and customer pages.

9. Launch Sequence

  • Day 1: Setup + Upload + Schedule
  • Day 2: Integrations + Widgets
  • Day 3: Social launch + Sponsors
  • Day 7: Merch + Memberships

10. Daily Operations

Check analytics, update playlists, approve ads, schedule shows, promote events.

11. Weekly Operations

Drop mixes, host live shows, publish analytics, add vendors.

12. Monthly Operations

Host Beltline events, release new playlists, publish monthly analytics.
